## Lessons Learned
- **Vision Overhead**: Base64 encoding for vision prompts is memory-heavy; buffering is key.
- **FS Events**: `WRITE` events are superior to `CLOSE_WRITE` for handling slow network drives or browser downloads.
- **In-Context Learning**: A simple "Affinities" table is highly effective at steering AI without retraining or complex fine-tuning.
